Divine Waltz

Divine Waltz is a deeply personal project that began as a poem and evolved into a rich, multi-layered animated film. The work explores universal themes of growth, transformation, and the journey toward self-realization through a unique blend of visual storytelling techniques. This poetic work combines traditional filmmaking techniques with motion capture to create a deeply personal and visually stunning narrative.

The project began with a written poem that served as the emotional and thematic foundation. From there, I developed the visual language and animation style that would best serve the narrative's emotional core.

Key creative elements include:

  1. Poetic Adaptation – Translating written poetry into visual metaphors and animated sequences.
  2. Mixed Media Approach – Combining 2D and 3D animation techniques for rich, layered visuals.
  3. Emotional Storytelling – Using motion capture to create authentic, human-like character performances.
